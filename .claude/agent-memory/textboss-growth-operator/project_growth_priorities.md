Assessed 2026-04-13. Based on offer-market fit, conversion intent, distribution efficiency, and current infrastructure state.

## Channel Ranking

1. Reddit — organic community seeding (highest priority, lowest cost, fastest feedback)
2. SEO + programmatic landing pages (highest long-term leverage, zero current infrastructure)
3. LinkedIn — founder/operator content (best audience-product fit for B2B positioning)
4. Short-form video (Twitter/X + LinkedIn) — scenario-driven demo content
5. Email list building via lead magnet (foundational infrastructure missing entirely)

## Highest-leverage move this week
Build and launch a lead magnet opt-in: a free "Client Message Playbook" PDF (scope creep, pricing pushback, payment reminders, no-show follow-ups) delivered via email. This creates the email list that currently does not exist, gives the business a warm nurture asset, and is directly on-brand.

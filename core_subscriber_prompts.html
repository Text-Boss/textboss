Appointment Scheduling & Confirmation8 prompts

#1Initial Appointment Request
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, this is {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}} from {{BUSINESS_NAME}}. I'd like to schedule a time for us to meet and discuss your project. Are you available this week on Tuesday or Thursday afternoon? Let me know what works best and I'll send through a confirmation. You can also reach me at {{BUSINESS_PHONE}} if you'd like to chat first.
#2Appointment Confirmation
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, just confirming your appointment with {{BUSINESS_NAME}} on [DATE] at [TIME]. Please arrive 5 minutes early so we can get started on time. If anything changes, let me know at least 24 hours in advance at {{BUSINESS_EMAIL}} or {{BUSINESS_PHONE}}. Looking forward to seeing you.
#3Appointment Reminder - 24 Hours
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, this is a reminder that your appointment with {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}} at {{BUSINESS_NAME}} is tomorrow at [TIME]. If you need to reschedule, please let me know today so I can offer the slot to another client. See you then.
#4Reschedule Request to Client
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, I need to reschedule our meeting originally set for [DATE]. I apologize for the inconvenience. Could we move to [NEW DATE] at [TIME] instead? If that doesn't suit, let me know your availability and I'll lock in a new time. Thanks for your understanding. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}, {{BUSINESS_NAME}}
#5Booking Link Follow-Up
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, thanks for your interest in working with {{BUSINESS_NAME}}. You can book a consultation directly at {{WEBSITE}} or reply to this message with a time that works for you. I have availability this week on [DAYS]. Talk soon. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}
#6Confirm Rescheduled Appointment
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, this confirms your rescheduled appointment with {{BUSINESS_NAME}} on [NEW DATE] at [TIME]. Same location and same agenda as originally planned. If anything else comes up, just reach me at {{BUSINESS_PHONE}}. See you there.
#7Walk-In Availability Response
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, thanks for reaching out. We don't take walk-ins at {{BUSINESS_NAME}} but I'd be happy to book you in. The next available slot is [DATE] at [TIME]. Would that work for you? You can also check availability at {{WEBSITE}}. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}
#8Post-Appointment Follow-Up
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, thanks for coming in today. It was great to meet with you. As discussed, I'll send through [DELIVERABLE] by [DATE]. If you have any questions in the meantime, don't hesitate to reach out at {{BUSINESS_EMAIL}}. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}, {{BUSINESS_NAME}}

Payment Reminders8 prompts

#9First Invoice Notice - Friendly

#10Payment Due Today
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, this is a reminder that your payment of [AMOUNT] for {{BUSINESS_NAME}} is due today. If you've already made the payment, thank you and please disregard this message. If not, please arrange payment at your earliest convenience. The invoice is attached to the original email or I can resend it now. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}
#11Second Notice - Overdue 7 Days
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, I'm following up on invoice [#INVOICE] for [AMOUNT], which was due on [DUE DATE]. It's now 7 days overdue. Could you please arrange payment or let me know if there's an issue? I'm happy to work out a solution. You can reach me at {{BUSINESS_EMAIL}}. Thanks. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}, {{BUSINESS_NAME}}
#12Payment Received - Thank You
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, just confirming I've received your payment of [AMOUNT] for invoice [#INVOICE]. Thank you for taking care of that. If you need a receipt, let me know and I'll send one through. Appreciate your business. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}, {{BUSINESS_NAME}}
#13Deposit Request Before Work Begins
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, before I begin work on your project, I require a deposit of [AMOUNT] as outlined in our agreement. Once the deposit is received, I'll schedule your start date and send through a timeline. Payment details are in the invoice I've just emailed. Let me know if you have any questions. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}, {{BUSINESS_NAME}}
#14Milestone Payment Due
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, we've completed [MILESTONE] on your project and the milestone payment of [AMOUNT] is now due. I've sent the invoice to your email. Once payment is received, I'll proceed with the next phase. Let me know if you have questions. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}, {{BUSINESS_NAME}}
#15Final Balance Due on Completion
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, your project is now complete and the final balance of [AMOUNT] is due. The invoice has been sent to your email. Once payment clears, I'll release all final files and deliverables. Thanks for working with {{BUSINESS_NAME}}. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}
#16Payment Plan Check-In
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, this is a reminder that your next installment of [AMOUNT] is due on [DATE] as per our agreed payment plan. If you need to adjust the schedule, please let me know before the due date so we can sort it out. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}, {{BUSINESS_NAME}}

#19Follow-Up After Quote Sent

#20Follow-Up - Awaiting Materials
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, I'm ready to continue work on your project but I'm still waiting on [MATERIALS/CONTENT/ASSETS] from your end. Could you send those through when you get a chance? It'll help me keep things on schedule. Thanks. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}, {{BUSINESS_NAME}}
#21Follow-Up - Approval Needed
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, just following up on the [DELIVERABLE] I sent for your review. I need your approval before I can proceed to the next stage. If you'd like any changes, let me know and I'll revise. Otherwise, a quick thumbs up works. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}, {{BUSINESS_NAME}}
#22Final Follow-Up - Closing the Loop

Just reach out to {{BUSINESS_EMAIL}} or {{BUSINESS_PHONE}}. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}, {{BUSINESS_NAME}}

Politely Declining Requests6 prompts

#29Service Not Offered
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, thanks for thinking of me for that. Unfortunately, [SERVICE] isn't something {{BUSINESS_NAME}} offers. I'd recommend reaching out to a specialist in that area. If I can think of anyone to refer you to, I'll send their details through. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}
#30Fully Booked
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, I appreciate your interest in working with {{BUSINESS_NAME}}. Unfortunately, I'm fully booked until [DATE]. I can add you to my waitlist and reach out when a spot opens up. Alternatively, I can recommend someone who may be able to help sooner. Let me know your preference. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}
#31Declining a Discount Request
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, I appreciate you asking. My pricing at {{BUSINESS_NAME}} reflects the quality and time that goes into every project, and I keep my rates consistent for all clients. The quoted price of [AMOUNT] is my best rate for this scope of work. Let me know if you'd like to go ahead. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}
#32Declining Weekend/After-Hours Work
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, I received your message. My business hours at {{BUSINESS_NAME}} are [HOURS], Monday to Friday. I'll get back to you first thing on the next business day. For urgent matters, you can email {{BUSINESS_EMAIL}} and I'll prioritize it when I'm back online. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}
#33Declining a Rush Job
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, I'd love to help but I'm unable to turn this around by [DATE] without compromising quality. The earliest I could deliver is [REALISTIC DATE]. If the rush timeline is essential, I do offer expedited rates at [RUSH RATE]. Let me know what works for you. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}, {{BUSINESS_NAME}}
#34Declining a Barter/Trade Request
Hi {{CLIENT_NAME}}, I appreciate the offer. At this time, {{BUSINESS_NAME}} only accepts standard payment for services. I'd still be happy to work with you at my quoted rate if you'd like to move forward. Let me know. - {{YOUR_FIRST_NAME}}

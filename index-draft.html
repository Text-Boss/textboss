// 09 — FAQ

Common questions.

Is this just email templates?

No. It's a structured communication system: templates for known patterns, a tier-gated AI assistant for real-time situations, and — for Pro and Black — a conversational AI that handles appointment booking end-to-end.

How does the AI Appointment Scheduler work?

The scheduler is a persistent conversational AI available to Pro and Black members. It engages clients in natural language to find a time, locks the booking, sends confirmation, and handles rebooking if plans change — without you managing the thread. Black members additionally get legally defensible scheduling language and no-show containment scripts.

Does the AI Scheduler integrate with my calendar?

The scheduling AI handles the conversational booking layer — the dialogue, confirmation, and follow-up. Calendar system integrations are on the roadmap. For now, confirmed times are delivered to you clearly so you can add them to your preferred system.

Why subscription instead of one-time purchase?

Client pressure patterns evolve. Your library and AI playbook needs to evolve with them. Subscription keeps it current — templates, AI behaviour, and scheduling flows all update when new patterns emerge.

Why not just use ChatGPT?

Generic AI will happily over-explain, introduce negotiation leakage, and book appointments without any awareness of risk level or legal defensibility. Text Boss is constrained by tier, structure, and risk level — including how your scheduling AI communicates.

Will this make me sound aggressive?

No. It makes you sound structured, calm, and decisive — in both client messages and scheduling interactions. Authority without emotional tone.

What happens if a client no-shows?

Black members get no-show containment scripts: controlled AI-drafted follow-ups that document the missed appointment and preserve your position — without escalating or conceding anything. Pro members receive standard rebooking flows.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. Cancel any time from your account. No lock-in periods, no cancellation fees. Your access continues until the end of your current billing cycle.

Is my data private?

Conversations are processed to generate responses and are not stored beyond your session. No conversation data is shared or sold.

Can I customise the templates?

Templates are designed to be send-ready. You can adapt any template before sending — they're copy-paste starting points, not locked scripts.
